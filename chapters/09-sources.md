## Ongoing reading stack

Every week, scan:

- FSC and KoFIU releases
- Bank of Korea releases on digital currency and tokenization experiments
- White House, Treasury, and major U.S. regulator updates where relevant
- EU and MiCA implementation updates
- HKMA and MAS releases
- Circle, Visa, Stripe, Coinbase, Fireblocks, Chainalysis, and major custodians or infra providers

## How to build your own source hierarchy

Use this order:

1. Official regulator or government source
2. Official company documentation or press release
3. Primary market data source
4. Serious research report
5. Everything else

## Your monthly review ritual

Once a month, answer:

- Which jurisdiction became more open or more restrictive?
- Which product category gained clearer commercial traction?
- Which new buyer type appeared?
- Which of my previous assumptions no longer looks right?

That is how you keep this book alive instead of static.
