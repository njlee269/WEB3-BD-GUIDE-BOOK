# 12-Week Study Curriculum

## How to study

Study in three modes every week:

- concept mode: understand the market and frameworks
- operating mode: turn concepts into account strategy and deal logic
- output mode: write, model, or present something so the knowledge becomes usable

You should produce artifacts weekly.

That is how you convert knowledge into career leverage.

## Weekly cadence

- Monday: market reading and note consolidation
- Tuesday: one deep concept topic
- Wednesday: one company or protocol case study
- Thursday: one account strategy or business model teardown
- Friday: one memo or LinkedIn-quality note
- Saturday: discussion, review, or language practice for client-facing communication

## Week 1: Industry map

Learn:

- market structure of crypto and digital assets
- who the major buyer types are

Learn:

- how stablecoin settlement works

## Detailed topic guide

If you want depth, organize your note system around these topic buckets.

## A. Stablecoins

Know:

- issuer models
- reserve model basics
- settlement flow
- wallet and custody choices
- on-off ramp design
- corridor economics
- merchant and treasury use cases

Be able to explain:

- why stablecoins matter even to users who do not care about crypto
- why bank integration and compliance are part of the product

## B. Tokenization

Know:

- legal wrapper logic
- difference between a token and a security claim
- transfer restrictions
- servicing and reporting
- secondary trading challenges

Be able to explain:

- when tokenization creates actual value and when it is mostly cosmetic

## C. Compliance and regulation

Know:

- KYC and KYB basics
- travel rule
- sanctions and wallet screening
- licensing perimeter
- securities risk

Be able to explain:

- how regulatory uncertainty changes sales cycle design

## D. Payments and treasury

Know:

- settlement timing
- liquidity needs
- pre-funding
- FX spread
- reconciliation
- treasury workflow

Be able to explain:

- where the actual savings come from in a cross-border workflow

## E. Enterprise sales

Know:

- procurement logic
- security review
- legal review
- executive sponsorship
- pilot design

Be able to explain:

- why enterprise deals fail and how to de-risk them early

## Your reading discipline

Do not just consume crypto media.

Read across:

- official regulator releases
- bank and payment company announcements
- issuer and custody documentation
- serious market structure analysis
- earnings calls and investor letters from adjacent fintech companies

## The rule

Every week, convert knowledge into one of:

- memo
- presentation
- account brief
- market map
- pitch rewrite

Otherwise the learning will remain passive.
